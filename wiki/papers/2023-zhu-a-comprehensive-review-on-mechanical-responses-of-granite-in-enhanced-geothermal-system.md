# A Comprehensive Review on Mechanical Responses of Granite in Enhanced Geothermal Systems (EGSs).

## One-line Summary
该综述系统汇总了不同热冲击（水冷、液氮冷却等）、热循环及围压条件下花岗岩的力学响应，通过微观结构分析揭示宏观力学参数退化的机理，为增强型地热系统（EGS）的井壁稳定和储层改造提供可靠参数依据。

## Research Question
在增强型地热系统（EGS）采热过程中，高温花岗岩储层经受不同工作流体（水、LN₂等）的快速冷却（热冲击）和循环热应力。本文要回答的核心问题是：花岗岩的力学响应（抗压强度、弹性模量、抗拉强度、泊松比、破坏模式）如何随加热温度、冷却方式、热循环次数和围压变化？其宏观劣化与微观结构演化之间存在怎样的关联？

## Study Area / Data
数据源自对中英文文献中花岗岩力学试验的系统汇总。花岗岩样品来自全球多个地点，以中国花岗岩为主（33组），包括平邑、郑州、松辽、日照、随州、南安等典型地质单元[Zhu 2023, pp. 4-6 Table 2]。国外花岗岩包括澳大利亚Strathbogie、Harcourt，伊比利亚半岛等地。试验涵盖了从室温到800 ℃的加热，冷却方式有水、液氮、空气及CO₂，以及不同循环次数（最多30次）的处理。力学试验类型包括单轴压缩、三轴压缩、巴西劈裂和卸载围压试验。

## Methods
### 文献数据收集
系统检索并整理了涉及EGS热冲击后花岗岩力学响应的试验研究，包含多篇中文文献。重点提取加热冷却处理参数、原始力学性质、归一化后的强度与变形参数[Zhu 2023, pp. 4-6]。

### 热处理方法
- 加热：以中等速率（多为5 ℃/min）升温至目标温度（最高800 ℃），恒温保持至少1小时以确保温度均匀[Zhu 2023, p. 4]。
- 冷却：用水、液氮或空气快速冷却至室温；液氮气化温度约‑196 ℃，产生更剧烈的热冲击[Zhu 2023, pp. 4-5]。循环冷却时重复加热‑冷却过程。

### 微观结构观测
对处理后试样制备薄片，利用光学显微镜、SEM和X射线CT观察晶内、晶间微裂纹的萌生、扩展与连接[Zhu 2023, p. 6]。

### 力学试验
在室温下开展单轴/三轴压缩、巴西劈裂试验，记录应力‑应变曲线、强度、弹性模量、抗拉强度和泊松比，并辅以声发射（AE）和数字图像相关（DIC）技术监测破坏过程[Zhu 2023, pp. 6-7]。部分研究进行了卸载围压试验以模拟钻井卸载条件[Zhu 2023, p. 7]。

### 归一化处理
为消除不同花岗岩自身差异，采用归一化值（热处理后参数/室温参数）进行比较[Zhu 2023, pp. 7-9]。

## Key Findings
1. **强度与弹性模量**：各类花岗岩的归一化单轴抗压强度（UCS）、抗拉强度（σₜ）和弹性模量（E）随加热温度升高呈线性下降（平均R²>0.97）。围压显著提高热损伤花岗岩的三轴抗压强度和弹性模量，高围压下强度衰减远小于单轴情况（600 ℃时归一化三轴强度仍可达0.869，800 ℃时0.892，而单轴强度仅0.278）[Zhu 2023, pp. 9-11]。
2. **热循环效应**：力学参数在最初几次（约5次）热循环中明显下降，随后降幅趋缓并趋于稳定[Zhu 2023, pp. 11, 15-17]。
3. **应力‑应变曲线特征**：热冲击和热循环使压密阶段非线性变形更显著，岩石延性增强；围压则抑制该非线性特征，使破坏前峰后曲线更陡[Zhu 2023, pp. 9, 10]。
4. **破坏模式转变**：随温度升高和围压增大，花岗岩由轴向劈裂破坏过渡为剪切破坏甚至多重剪切破坏；卸载路径促进横向张拉裂纹，导致更复杂的剪切破坏[Zhu 2023, pp. 12-13]。
5. **微观机理**：不同矿物热膨胀各向异性及α‑β石英相变（573 ℃）引发晶间、晶内微裂纹。水冷和LN₂冷加剧微裂纹密度与宽度，LN₂冷却造成的损伤最严重。微裂纹的萌生、扩展和连接是宏观力学参数劣化的直接原因[Zhu 2023, pp. 13-15]。

## Limitations
- Majority of studies used pre‑heated specimens tested at room temperature, not in‑situ high‑temperature and high‑pressure conditions [Zhu 2023, p. 15].
- Limited triaxial compressive data for granites after rapid cooling (water/LN₂), and almost no cyclic cooling under triaxial conditions [Zhu 2023, p. 11].
- CO₂‑cooling mechanical tests are limited to temperatures below 300 °C (200–240 °C) due to equipment complexity, and few tests exist for cyclic ScCO₂ cooling [Zhu 2023, pp. 15-17].
- The dataset heavily leans on Chinese granites (33 groups), which may not fully represent the global HDR lithology diversity [Zhu 2023, p. 5-6].
- Effect of heating duration was neglected, assuming sufficient damage occurs within 2 h for high temperatures [Zhu 2023, p. 4].
- Microstructural observations are post‑treatment; real‑time crack evolution under coupled thermal‑mechanical loads was not addressed.

## Assumptions / Conditions
- Heating rate of 5 °C/min is assumed to avoid thermal shock during heating; soaking time ≥1 h ensures uniform temperature distribution [Zhu 2023, p. 4].
- One thermal shock cycle is defined as heating to target temperature and cooling to room temperature [Zhu 2023, p. 5].
- The use of normalized mechanical parameters (ratio to room‑temperature value) allows cross‑comparison among different granites with varying initial properties [Zhu 2023, pp. 7-8].
- Micro‑defect development is considered the primary control on macro‑mechanical changes; mineral expansion anisotropy data (Clark 1966) are assumed applicable [Zhu 2023, p. 13].

## Key Variables / Parameters
- Normalized uniaxial compressive strength: UCS_T/UCS_0
- Normalized elastic modulus: E/E_0
- Normalized tensile strength: σₜ/σₜ₀
- Normalized Poisson’s ratio: ν_T/ν₀
- Heating temperature (T, °C)
- Number of thermal cycles (N)
- Cooling medium: water, LN₂, air, CO₂
- Confining stress (σ₃, MPa)
- Crack initiation stress (σ_ci) and crack damage stress (σ_cd) from AE

## Reusable Claims
1. After thermal shocks, the average normalized UCS of granites decreases linearly with heating temperature, with correlation coefficient R²=0.9876; at 800 °C, UCS_T/UCS_0 ≈ 0.278 [Zhu 2023, pp. 9-10]. The claim holds for data compiled from >30 granite types under water or LN₂ cooling, provided the heating rate was moderate and specimens were tested unconfined at room temperature.
2. Confining stress greatly mitigates the loss of compressive strength and elastic modulus at high temperatures. At 800 °C, normalized triaxial compressive strength remains 0.869–0.892, while uniaxial strength drops to ~0.278 [Zhu 2023, p. 11]. This claim is based on limited triaxial data; most triaxial tests used air cooling, not rapid water/LN₂.
3. Mechanical degradation occurs predominantly in the first ~5 thermal cycles; further cycles cause negligible additional reduction in UCS, E, and tensile strength [Zhu 2023, pp. 11, 15, 20]. This behavior is observed under both water and LN₂ cyclic cooling for temperatures up to 600 °C.
4. With increasing temperature and confining pressure, the failure mode of granite transitions from axial splitting to shear failure and multiple shear failures. Under unloading conditions, lateral tensile stress promotes complex shear cracking [Zhu 2023, pp. 12-13, 15]. The trend is consistent across several Chinese granites (Xuzhou, Suizhou, Macheng).
5. LN₂ cooling induces greater microcracking and larger reductions in strength and tensile strength than water cooling, due to its ultralow temperature (−196 °C) and higher thermal gradient [Zhu 2023, pp. 15, 12]. This claim is supported by direct comparison of Luhui and Shandong granites.
6. The α–β quartz phase transition at 573 °C causes a volume expansion of ~0.45%, strongly promoting microcrack initiation and contributing to the sharp decline in mechanical properties above this temperature [Zhu 2023, pp. 13-14].

## Open Questions
- 在实时高温高压条件下，花岗岩的力学响应规律如何？现有试验多以降温后室温测试为主，不能完全反映原位状态[Zhu 2023, p. 15]。
- 超临界CO₂作为工质时，循环冷却对花岗岩的长期力学影响缺乏试验数据，且现有试验温度多限于300 °C以下[Zhu 2023, pp. 15-17]。
- 不同冷却速率（如水冷的平均307.32 °C/min与空气的7.02 °C/min）对力学响应的影响尚需系统量化[Zhu 2023, pp. 4-5]。
- 热‑水‑力‑化学多场耦合作用下微裂纹的时空演化规律仍需深入研究。
- 如何利用热冲击引起的力学劣化优化储层改造，同时避免井壁失稳，尚缺少定量模型支撑。

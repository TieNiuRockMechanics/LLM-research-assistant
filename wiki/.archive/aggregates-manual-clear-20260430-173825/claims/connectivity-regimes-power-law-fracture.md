# Claim: For fracture networks with a power-law length distribution, three connectivity regimes exist depending on the exponent a: small-fracture-dominated (a>3), crossover (1<a<3), and large-fracture-dominated (a<1); the percolation threshold scales as L^{-2} for a>3 and as L^{a-1} for 1<a<3.

# Coupled thermal-hydraulic-mechanical processes in enhanced geothermal systems

## Synthesis

EGS performance is governed by the interplay of fluid flow, heat transport, and rock deformation; thermoporoelastic effects, fracture activation, and induced seismicity are key coupled phenomena.

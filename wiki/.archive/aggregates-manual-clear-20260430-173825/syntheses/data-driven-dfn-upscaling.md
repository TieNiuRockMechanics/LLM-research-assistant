# Statistical and Data-Driven Approaches for DFN Model Construction and Upscaling

## Synthesis

Recent advances integrate machine learning (GANs, neural networks, CMA-ES) with traditional stochastic DFN methods. Generative models can reconstruct and upscale fracture networks from single outcrop images, while Bayesian inversion (rjMCMC) combines multiple data types (hydraulic, thermal, microseismic) to characterize fracture properties and reduce uncertainty. Hybrid approaches embedding matrix flow are necessary for fractured-porous systems.

# Thermal damage mechanisms in rocks under high temperature and rapid cooling

## Synthesis

Elevated temperature and subsequent rapid cooling (water, liquid nitrogen, dry ice) induce microcracking through thermal stress, phase transitions, and mineral decomposition, degrading mechanical properties and increasing permeability.

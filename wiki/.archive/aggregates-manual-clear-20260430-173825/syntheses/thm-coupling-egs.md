# Coupled Thermo-Hydro-Mechanical (THM) Processes in Enhanced Geothermal Systems

## Synthesis

Heat extraction from fractured geothermal reservoirs involves strong feedback between thermal, hydraulic, and mechanical processes. Cold fluid injection induces thermal contraction, alters fracture aperture, and can trigger seismicity. Poroelastic and thermoporoelastic effects, along with contact mechanics, are critical to predict reservoir performance. Numerical models coupling these processes are essential for designing sustainable EGS operations.

# Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) for DFN Generation

## Summary

A deep learning approach (e.g., WGAN-GP, SinGAN) that learns the statistical distribution of fracture patterns from training images and generates new, statistically similar fracture networks with dimensionality reduction.
